Founder’s Office Club (FOC) Job Board

About us: Founder’s Office Club (FOC) is an exclusive community for folks in the Founder’s office, CoS, EIR, or Office of CxO roles. Our goal is to establish a tightly connected community where we can cultivate enduring and significant connections while uncovering valuable insights, tools, resources, and accessing unique experiences. Get a sneak peek at our Members' Directory: https://tinyurl.com/FOC-Member-Directory. Link to the waitlist for joining the FOC community: https://bit.ly/FOC-Member-Waitlist. Want to support a thriving generalist's community? Explore partnership opportunities with FOC by filling up the form here: https://tally.so/r/wAW4Dl. Explore the Community: https://linktr.ee/foc_community. Job Portal: Job Providers: Are you looking to hire top-tier talent for your Founder's Office, Chief of Staff, Entrepreneur in residence or CXO roles? Please fill out the form here. Job Seekers: If you're applying for any of the roles listed below, please consider using us as a reference.
